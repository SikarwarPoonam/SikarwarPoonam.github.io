# What is a Band Gap?

The band gap is the energy difference between the valence band of electrons and the conduction band.

## Why it matters for Solar
In solar cells, photons with energy greater than the band gap ($h\nu > E_g$) can create electron-hole pairs.



* **Silicon:** 1.1 eV (Indirect) x^2
* **Gallium Arsenide:** 1.42 eV (Direct)
